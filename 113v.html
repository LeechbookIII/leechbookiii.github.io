viiii. Wiþ hwostan, wyl marubian on wætre godne dæl. Geswet hwon sele drincan scenc ful.
viiii. Against a cough, boil a good deal of white horehound in water. Sweeten a little. Give a cup full to drink.
Eft, marubian swiðe wyl on hunige. Do hwon buteran on. Sele .iii. snæda oþþe .iiii. etan on neahtnestig. Be sup scenc fulne mid wearmes þæs ærran drences.
Afterwards, boil white horehound vigorously in honey. Put in a little butter. Give three or four morsels to eat after a night's fast26. Sup a cupful with [it] of the warmed former drink.
