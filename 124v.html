If you wish to cure that person, consider his bearing and be aware of which sex he is. If it is a man and he looks up when you first look at him and his face is yellow-black, you might cure that man completely if he is not [like this] too long therein38. If it is a woman and she looks down when you first look at her and her face is dark red, you might also cure her. If she is a day longer than twelve months and her face is such- like, then you might better her for a while and might not completey cure her.
