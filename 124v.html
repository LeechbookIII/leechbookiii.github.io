and .iii. cropleaces and .iii. sealtes and hæbbe him scenc fulne ealað and drype þriwa halig wæter on besupe ælce snæd gereste hine siþþan do þis .viiii. morgenas and .viiii. niht him biþ sona sel.
and three of leek and three of salt and have for him a cup full of ale and drip holy water three times into [it]. Eat each morsel. Let him rest after. Do this nine mornings and nine nights. Soon he is well.
Gif him biþ ælfsogoþa him beoþ þa eagan geolwe þær hi reade beon sceoldon.
If he is elf-sucked, the eyes of [the person] are be yellow where they should be red.
Gif þu þone mon lacnian wille þænc his gebæra and wite hwilces hades he sie gif hit biþ wæpned man and locað up þonne þu hine ærest sceawast and se andwlita biþ geolwe blac þone mon þu meaht gelacnian æltæwlice gif he ne biþ þær on to lange gif hit biþ wif and locað niþer þonne þu hit ærest sceawast and hire andwlita biþ reade wan þæt þu miht eac gelacnian gif hit bið dægþerne leng on þonne .xii. monaþ and sio onsyn biþ þyslicu þonne meaht þu hine gebetan to hwile and ne meaht hwæþere æltæwlice gelacnian.
If you wish to cure that person, consider his bearing and be aware of which sex he is. If it is a man and he looks up when you first look at him and his face is yellow-black, you might cure that man perfectly if he is not therein too long37. If it is a woman and she looks down when you first look at her and her face is dark red, that you might also cure. If it is on [the person] a day's time longer than twelve months and his visage is such, then you might better him for a while and might not perfectly cure him.
Writ þis gewrit. Scriptum est rex regum et dominus dominantium byrnice beronice lurlure iehe aius aius aius Sanctus Sanctus Sanctus Dominus deus sabooth amen alleluia.
Write this writ, "Scriptum est rex regum et dominus dominantsu byrnice beronice lurlure iehe aius aius aius sanctus sanctus sanctus dominus deus sabooth amen alleluiah."
Sing þis ofer þam drence and þam gewrite. Deus omnipotens pater domini nostri iesu cristi per inpositionem huius scriptura expelle afamulo tuo Nomine omnem impetuum castalidum de spite. de capillis.
Sing this over the drink and the writ, "Deus omnis pater domini nostri iesu Christi per impositionem huius scriptura expelle afamulotuo non omnem impetuum castiliduum decapite decapillis
37. That is, if the person has been elf-sucked for too long. ↩
