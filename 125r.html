Wyrc þonne drenc font wæter rudan. saluian. cassus. draconzan. þa smeþan wegbrædan niþewearde feferfugian. diles crop. garleaces .iii. clufe. finul. wermod. lufestice. elehtre. ealre emfela writ .iii. crucem mid oleum infirmorum and cweþ pax tibi.
Then make a drink of fountain water, pennyroyal, sage, reed, dragon aurum, the weary greater plantain, the lower part of feverfue, dill's top, three cloves of garlic, fennel, wormwood, lovage, lupine. Write three crosses with oil of unction and say paxtibi.
Nim þonne þæt gewrit writ crucem mid ofer þam drince sing þis þær ofer. Deus omnipotens pater domini nostri iesu cristi per inpositionem huius scriptura et per gustum huius expelle diabolum afamulo tuo. Nomine and credo and pater noster. wæt þæt gewrit on þam drence and write crucem mid him on ælcum lime and cweþ signum crucis cristi conseruate inuitam eternam amen. gif þe ne lyste hat hine selfne oþþe swa gesubne swa he gesibb oft hæbbe and senige swa he sel oft cunne þes cræft mæg wiþ ælcre feondes costunge.
Then take that writing, write crosses with [it] over the drink and sing this there over, "Deus omnipotens pater domini nostri iesu Christ, per impositionem huius scriptura et huius expelle diabolum a famulo tuo, NAME" and the creed and the Pater Noster. Wet that writing in the drink and write crosses with it on each limb and say, "Signum crucis Christi conserva te invitam aeternam amen." If you allow it, bid himself or such relative as he often has and sign as he oft well can. This craft may [work] against every temptation of the fiend.
Lxiii. Gif mon biþ on wæter ælf adle þonne beoþ him þa hand næglas wonne and þa eagan tearige and wile locian niþer
Lxiii. If a person is in the water-elf-disease, then the nails of his hand are dark and the eyes teary, and he will look down.
38. A Latin lorica, a prayer that asks for protection over each part of the body. ↩
