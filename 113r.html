Old English
Modern English
IIII. Wiþ toþ ece ceow pipor gelome mid þam toþum him biþ sona sel.
IIII. Against a toothache chew pepper often with the teeth. Soon he is well.
Eft seoð beolenan moran on strangum ecede oþþe on þæt ine sete on þone saran toþ and hwilum ceowe mid þy saran toþe he bið hal.
Afterwards, boil henbane root in strong vinegar or in wine. Set on the sore tooth and chew for a while with the sore tooth. He is whole.
Gif þa teþ synd hole ceow boþenes moran mid ecede on þa healfe.
If the teeth have holes, chew rosemary root with vinegar on that part.
v. Wiþ þinnan tobrocenum muðe nim plum treowes leaf wyl on wine and swile mide þone muþ.
v. Against an inwardly broken mouth take the leaf of the plum tree. Boil in wine and swill [it] with the mouth.
vi. Wiþ ceocadle nim þone hweorfan þe wif mid swinnað bind on his sweoran mid wyllenan þræde and swile innan mid hate gate meolce him biþ sel.
vi. Against cheek disease take the whorl a woman spins with. Bind [it] on his neck with woolen thread and swill in the mouth with hot goat's milk. He is well.
Wið ceol wærce adelf ær sunnan upgange wegbrædan bind on hi sweoran.
Against a cheek pain, dig up greater plantain before sunrise. Bind it on his neck.
Eft bærn swealwan to duste and meng wiþ feldbeon hunig sele him etan gelome.
Afterwards, burn a swallow to dust and mix with field bee's honey. Give [it] to him to eat often.
vii. Wiþ heals wærce wyl neoþewearde netelan on oxan smerwe and on buteran þonne þone healswærc smire ða þeoh. gif þa þeoh wærce smire þone heals mid þære sealfe.
vii. Against neck pain, boil the lower part of nettle in the fat of an ox and in butter. Then, when the neck hurts, smear the thigh. If the thigh hurts, smear the neck with that salve.
Eft wyl niþewearde netelan on ecede do oxan geallan on þæt eced and þa wyrte of smire mid þone heals.
Afterwards, boil the lower part of nettle in vinegar. Put the bile of an ox in that vinegar and [take] the herbs out. Smear the neck with it.
viii. Wiþ bite wyrc sealfe. nim þas wyrte safenan
viii. Against cancer23 make a salve. Take the herbs sabine
